About Us

Welcome to Llanion Cove, located in the charming town of Pembroke Dock! We are your ultimate destination for thrilling sports and outdoor activities. Our center specializes in a variety of exhilarating experiences, including Stand Up Paddleboarding (SUP), kayaking, canoeing, mountain biking, and bushcraft.

Llanion Cove is surrounded by the natural beauty of Pembroke Dock, providing the perfect backdrop for your adventure-filled journey. Our dedicated team of knowledgeable instructors is passionate about sharing their expertise and ensuring that every moment you spend with us is filled with excitement and enjoyment.

Experience the thrill of Stand Up Paddleboarding (SUP) as you glide gracefully across the shimmering waters. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced paddler, our SUP sessions cater to all skill levels, allowing you to explore the stunning coastline and its hidden gems.

Discover the freedom of kayaking and canoeing as you navigate through pristine waterways, observing the breathtaking scenery and wildlife along the way. Our well-maintained kayaks and canoes, combined with expert guidance, ensure a safe and memorable experience for all.

Whether you're a local resident or visiting Pembroke Dock, Llanion Cove invites you to embark on an unforgettable journey of sports and outdoor exploration. Join us at our center and let the natural beauty of Pembroke Dock be your playground. Get ready to create memories, challenge yourself, and experience the thrill of Llanion Cove's sports activities.
