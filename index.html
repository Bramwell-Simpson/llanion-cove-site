About Us

Llanion Cove lies within a picturesque ancient woodland on the banks of the river Cleddau, providing a serene escape for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty. The surrounding trails offer opportunities for leisurely walks, inviting visitors to explore the peaceful forested surroundings and immerse themselves in the calming ambiance of the area.

Our mission is to provide a quality residential experience with safe, meaningful, fun activities implemented by Licensed activity providers while giving comfortable, safe accommodation and wholesome enjoyable food. We aim to become part of the community working with local groups to improve our area for all ages and we also offer daily activities for local people such as Kayak hire and The climbing wall.

Our Facilities

Llanion Cove is a unique facility set in the heart of Pembrokeshire on the banks of the famous river Cleddau. We have accommodation that can offer up to 80 spaces in bunk house style. We can cater for adults and children of all abilities with one block designed especially for people with disabilities. For schools, clubs and individuals seeking adventure activities, we are able to offer an extensive range of exciting and challenging activities both land and water based.

Our Activities

Llanion Cove can provide bespoke residential activity packages to schools and other groups of young people. With many years experience of organising residential activity breaks for schools and with close links with various Local Authorities, Llanion Cove can put together a totally bespoke package to suit your requirements following consultation regarding your National Curriculum studies based in this magnificent location for future school residential trips.

A typical activity programme can include adventure activities such as Sailing, Canoeing, Climbing, Orienteering, Paddle boarding, Forest schools, and many more with access to artificial walls and natural crags, lakes, rivers and the sea, woodlands and beaches. With our offices and accommodation overlooking the Cleddau estuary, it's no surprise we spend a lot of time out on the water! Fieldwork studies can include, rock pooling, pond dipping, bushcraft, specific history and geography studies. Our team include Geography, PE and Science teachers in addition to the specialist activity instructors.
